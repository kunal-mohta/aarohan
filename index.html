An Educational Initiative by BITS Pilani
  • Apogee 2018
    AAROHAN is a national level examination crafted and conducted by the students of BITS Pilani in schools. The examination is conducted for classes IX, X, XI and XII. The aim of this examination is to inculcate scientific temperament and get the coming generation interested towards scientific and technical development studies. All that only at a cost of ₹150 per student.

  • Aarohan

    A wonderful learning experience

    Apart from a rst-hand exposure to a national level exam and an opportunity to compete with students from schools across India, Aarohan has a lot more to o er. You will get an ‘All India Rank’(AIR) and a certi cate of participation. The school and city toppers will get a certi cate of merit stating the same and would also get exciting prizes according to their ranks. The nal winners will be invited to BITS Pilani’s technical fest APOGEE. And on a materialistic and tangible scale, there’ll be exciting goodies and prizes as well as the opportunity to interact with the most progressive minds of the country.

  • Round 1 — Aarohan
    The paper will be Multiple Choice based.

    The duration of the paper will be two hours.

    The paper will have one unifying theme based on recent scientific breakthroughs and phenomena.

    The questions based on that will be entirely application based and not factual.

    The syllabus comprises of the syllabus of the students’ respective class. The students will have to get innovative with the basic formulae and improvise.

    The marking scheme will be revealed at the time of examination.

  • Round 2 — Aarohan Qrios
    Two students from each region would be selected for the nal round.

    Each student would be given a particular scienti c topic or a research area, one month prior to the second round.

    Students are supposed to gather knowledge, relevant data, notes, breakthroughs and current happenings related to that topic.

    Help from teachers is encouraged.

    Each student would have to present their topic which would be followed by constructive group discussion in the presence of a panel.
